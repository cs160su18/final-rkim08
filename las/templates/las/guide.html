LAS

What is LAS?

LAS stands for Life as Someone Else. Life as Someone Else is a platform that allows for one group of users - presumably students - to be able to see the daily activities that goes on during the life of a professional.

Who is this for?

Anyone can use LAS!

Why would you want to use LAS?

Professionals can use Life as Someone Else to write diary entries of their day. This can be a chance to organize their thoughts or to give back to the community by providing a more personal perspective of their jobs rather than the generic information provided on the internet.
Students will be able to use learn about a profession on a personal level as they read about the daily activities, working professionals do.

What categories do LAS offer?

LAS currently offers categories such as business, finance, health, law, and technology.

What is my collections?

My collections contains all of your information starting from the people you decided to follow, how many people follow your, your diary entries.
